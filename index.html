Our Journey

Started in Biratnagar with a vision close to my heart, Maya Boutique grew from a small studio into a space where tradition meets artistry. With an artist's eye and a deep love for tradition, I've always wanted to create pieces that reflect our heritage through delicate handwork, rich textures, and the timeless charm of traditional Nepali fabrics.

Each design reflects a story of culture, emotion, and elegance. I've poured my passion into every piece, believing that every stitch carries meaning and every embroidery preserves the beauty of our roots. Our creations have adorned local brides and graced fashion showcases, each one shaped by devotion, creativity, and cultural pride. Today, Maya Boutique stands not just as a brand, but as a deeply personal journey stitched with love, where culture and couture beautifully intertwine.

Knor Shrestha

Founder & Creative Designer
